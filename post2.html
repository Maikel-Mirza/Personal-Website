ClickGenius

This was a project I started while learning frontend JavaScript development. I was the sole creator/developer for this project.

The website serves as a comprehensive platform for businesses looking to enhance their online presence and marketing efforts.

Allows for users to market their business through social media, email, and text campaigns. Users can also create their own website through the platform.

This website serves as a digital hub for businesses seeking a wide range of digital marketing services. It educates visitors about your offerings, showcases past achievements, and facilitates communication to initiate potential collaborations.

I worked on this project for learning purposes but could develop into something with commercial value
